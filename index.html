More info about the festival

Explore new ideas, make friends, and make a difference to the world! Join a group of talented yoga and meditation instructors and likeminded people for a transformational experience. Whether you’re new to yoga or an experienced practitioner, you will absolutely love Yoga Day of Trondheim.

Yoga is an ancient, holistic approach to health and wellbeing that promotes harmony within oneself, between individuals, and with our natural environment.

Yoga exercises (asanas) help increase your flexibility, strengthen and tone muscles, balance hormones, cleanse and flush the internal systems of your body, calm your mind, and reduce stress. But yoga is not solely about exercise. 

Ancient yogis understood yoga as a complete lifestyle and worldview that can help you discover who you are, as well as your relationship to the world and nature. As such, it provides valuable insights into how to solve environmental challenges and promote peace in the individual, peace in communities, and peace in the world. The sages of India understood that the outer can change by changing the inner, and that the nature of our consciousness therefore affects the nature of our environment.

ASANA ROOM

11:00 - Let’s Yoga! Kick-off & Snacks

We welcome everyone to the International Yoga Day Festival of Trondheim with a brief introduction to Yoga. With a small teaser to the actual meaning of the word ‘Yoga’, a short session of gentle physical yoga exercises, and soothing and simple mantra meditation.

12:00 - Sun Salutations by Ruben

Surya Namaskar, or Sun Salutation, is the most known symbol of yoga practice. It is a flowing sequence of asanas (positions) that helps you connect the movement to your breath, while building heat and energizing the entire body. Our yoga teacher Ruben will guide you through several varieties of Sun Salutation - as always in a fun, relaxed, and inspirational manner.

13:00 - Pirates! Yoga for Kids by Therese

Yarr! Join us in the search for the hidden treasure of the secret island of Gauranga… a fast-paced and heart-warming kids yoga adventure about appreciation of the treasures of life. Fun for everyone! Your kids will be lead with care by our yoga teacher Therese, scout (speider) since early years.

14:00 - Chair Yoga by Mohini                                 

Not enough space to pull out the yoga mat in the office? There are still plenty of exercises you can do while sitting! Chair yoga is the perfect practice for office workers and students who want to get more things done and feel good in the meanwhile. We focus on balancing the most common causes of stress and illness from office work by opening up the chest, deepening the breath, and gently relaxing the tense muscles of the neck and shoulders.

14:30 - Deep Relaxation by Mohini

It is said that 15 minutes of Yoga Nidra relaxation may rejuvenate you more than 1 hour of sleep. On this class, you will get to lie down and be gently guided into a state of complete tranquility in mind and body. We also add mantra sounds to the relaxation, which takes you far beyond all worldly stress.

15:00 - Broga: Yoga for Dudes by Ruben

The 65 year-old lady next to you is doing headstands. The woman on the other side has wrapped herself up into a super-pretzel - while you are sweating bucket-loads just trying to touch your toes. It's high time to debunk the myths around yoga - it is actually a practice for both sexes, and for all kinds of bodies. The most basic postures will provide you with the foundation of flexibility and strength that every man needs. Welcome to a truly down-to-earth class for all the bros out there!

16:00 - Creative Yoga Flow by Mohini

A fun and soothing yoga class to inspire you and help you build physical and mental strength. Guided by the breath, we will flow through a harmonious sequence of asanas. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn from Mohini - with great enthusiasm for yoga and experience from many years of leading yoga teachers courses worldwide.

17:00 - Gentle Yin Yoga by Ruben

Yin Yoga is a deeply rejuvenating practice where we prolong the time spent in each asana (position). This stimulates the deeper tissues of the muscles to relax & recharge, while bringing the mind into a comfortable state of tranquility. With his soft voice and clear instructions, Ruben shares a a heart-warming experience and a personal challenge for you to land and center in your breath.

18:00 - Pre-premiere: ‘Who Are You?’

Pre-premier of the unreleased documentary ‘Who Are You?’. We’d like to take you for an exciting journey - a journey that could open up the doors to a much deeper understanding of life, love, and everything in between. The question ‘Who Am I?’ is not a speculational football to be kicked around at philosophy clubs - it is a real-life question with very practical implications. This documentary explores various scientific evidence all indicating that there is much more to us than just our bodies.

19:00 - Live Music Mantra Party

A melodious concert blending elements of a capella with mantra music. Our house band Northern Light Family shares a mood that truly inspires and warms the heart. This interactive acoustic mini-concert also rounds off the celebration of Yoga Day. We all head home with a big smile inside…

LIFESTYLE ROOM

12:00 - Mandala Workshop by Lisa & Therese

Learn & meditate: Creating a Mandala, an ancient archetypal symbol in the yoga system, is a powerful and fun way of self-expression. Using material from nature or pen and paper, invest a moment to allow your mind the freedom to express, without judgment, in a safe and supportive environment.

13:00 - Pranayama Breathing by Linn

‘Prana’ means ‘life-giving force’, ‘ayama’ means ‘extension’. Learning to control our breath can have an enormous effect on our physical and mental wellbeing. In this workshop you will learn simply yet highly effective breathing techniques that bring harmony to your entire system.

14:00 - Acoustic Kirtan Journey

Weaving together melody, music, and mantra, Kirtan is simultaneously a meditative yet energizing activity. It is the most powerful and enjoyable form of meditation. Come try it out!

15:00 - Yoga Beyond the Mat by Anja

Yoga is the wonderful science of living, loving, mindfulness, compassion, and humility. But we need to know the path upon which to travel in order to achieve this state. How do we build a mature and joyful personal practice that results in true happiness and enlightenment? With over 20 years of experience of meditation and study of Yogic truth, Anja Hammer will share inspiring insights into the actual meaning of ‘Yoga’. In a relaxed and inspiring way, she will show you why yoga is actually a practical and fun way to achieve a state of peace and harmony in life.

16:00 - Japa Mala Workshop by Frank

Make your own Japa Mala (beads) and practice with them - ‘Japa’ indicates a soft chanting of yoga sounds, or Mantra. Repeating these mantra sounds have the effect of drawing the mind away from the material dimension with all its stress, worries, etc., an effect that cannot be achieved by any material thing. With this practice, gradually your mind and heart will develop toward more tolerance, care, and love for others.

17:00 - Intro to Ayurveda by Linn

Ayurveda, or ‘the science of life’, is accepted as the most ancient health system of the world and aims toward leading the individual toward a state of optimal bodily and mental health. In Ayurveda, the food is considered our medicine and diet is therefore of great importance. Get a small insight to the holistic Ayurveda practice - and how it can really improve your life - in this enlightening workshop given by our meditation and lifestyle teacher Linn.
