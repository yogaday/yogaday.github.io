INSPIRATION
PEACE - UNITY

More info about the festival

Explore new ideas, make friends, and make a difference to the world! Join a group of talented yoga and meditation instructors and likeminded people for a transformational experience. Whether you’re new to yoga or an experienced practitioner, you will absolutely love this festival.

Yoga is an ancient, holistic approach to health and wellbeing that promotes harmony within oneself, between individuals, and with our natural environment.

Yoga exercises (asanas) help increase your flexibility, strengthen and tone muscles, balance hormones, cleanse and flush the internal systems of your body, calm your mind, and reduce stress. But yoga is not solely about exercise. 
Ancient yogis understood yoga as a complete lifestyle and worldview that can help you discover who you are, as well as your relationship to the world and nature. As such, it provides valuable insights into how to solve environmental challenges and promote peace in the individual, peace in communities, and peace in the world. The sages of India understood that the outer can change by changing the inner, and that the nature of our consciousness therefore affects the nature of our environment.

LIFESTYLE ROOM

10:00 - Kick-off in Main Area

We welcome everyone to the International Yoga Day Festival of Trondheim with a brief introduction to Yoga. With a small teaser to the actual meaning of the word ‘Yoga’, a short session of gentle physical yoga exercises, and soothing and simple mantra meditation.

11:00 - Pranayama Breathing & Deep Relaxation

Double class
11:00 Pranayama: ‘Prana’ means ‘life-giving force’, ‘ayama’ means ‘extension’. Learning to control our breath can have an enormous effect on our physical and mental wellbeing. In this workshop you will learn simply yet highly effective breathing techniques that bring harmony to your entire system.
11:30 Deep Relaxation: It is said that 15 minutes of Yoga Nidra relaxation may rejuvenate you more than 1 hour of sleep. On this class, you will get to lie down and be gently guided into a state of complete tranquility in mind and body. We also add mantra sounds to the relaxation, which takes you far beyond all worldly stress.

12:00 - Acoustic Kirtan Journey

Weaving together melody, music, and mantra, Kirtan is simultaneously a meditative yet energizing activity. It is the most powerful and enjoyable form of meditation. Come try it out!

13:00 - Vegetarian Inspiration | Magda

Take part of wonderful cooking tips on vegetarian food - the basis for a healthy and simple yogic diet.

14:00 - Yoga Beyond the Mat | Anja

Yoga is the wonderful science of living, loving, mindfulness, compassion, and humility. But we need to know the path upon which to travel in order to achieve this state. How do we build a mature and joyful personal practice that results in true happiness and enlightenment? With over 20 years of experience of meditation and study of Yogic truth, Anja Hammer will share inspiring insights into the actual meaning of ‘Yoga’. In a relaxed and inspiring way, she will show you why yoga is actually a practical and fun way to achieve a state of peace and harmony in life.

ASANA ROOM

10:00 - Kick-off in Main Area

We welcome everyone to the International Yoga Day Festival of Trondheim with a brief introduction to Yoga. With a small teaser to the actual meaning of the word ‘Yoga’, a short session of gentle physical yoga exercises, and soothing and simple mantra meditation.

11:00 - Creative Yoga Flow

A fun and soothing yoga class to inspire you and help you build physical and mental strength. Guided by the breath, we will flow through a harmonious sequence of asanas. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn from Ruben - with great enthusiasm for yoga and experience from over 8 years of regular practice of yoga asanas.

12:00 - Gentle Yin Yoga

Yin Yoga is a deeply rejuvenating practice where we prolong the time spent in each asana (position). This stimulates the deeper tissues of the muscles to relax & recharge, while bringing the mind into a comfortable state of tranquility. A heart-warming experience and a personal challenge for you to land and center in your breath.

13:00 - Broga: Yoga for Dudes

The 65 year-old lady next to you is doing headstands. The woman on the other side has wrapped herself up into a super-pretzel - while you are sweating bucket-loads just trying to touch your toes. It's high time to debunk the myths around yoga - it is actually a practice for both sexes, and for all kinds of bodies. The most basic postures will provide you with the foundation of flexibility and strength that every man needs. Welcome to a truly down-to-earth class for all the bros out there!

14:00 - Chair Yoga & Sun Salutations

Double class
14:00 Chair Yoga: Not enough space to pull out the yoga mat in the office? There are still plenty of exercises you can do while sitting! Chair yoga is the perfect practice for office workers and students who want to get more things done and feel good in the meanwhile. We focus on balancing the most common causes of stress and illness from office work by opening up the chest, deepening the breath, and gently relaxing the tense muscles of the neck and shoulders.
14:30 Sun Salutations: Surya Namaskar, or Sun Salutation, is the most known symbol of yoga practice. It is a flowing sequence of asanas (positions) that helps you connect the movement to your breath, while building heat and energizing the entire body

ART ROOM

10:00 - Kick-off in Main Area

We welcome everyone to the International Yoga Day Festival of Trondheim with a brief introduction to Yoga. With a small teaser to the actual meaning of the word ‘Yoga’, a short session of gentle physical yoga exercises, and soothing and simple mantra meditation.

11:00 - Nature Mandala | Therese

Learn, create, meditate: Making a Mandala, an ancient archetypal symbol in the yoga system, is a powerful and fun way of self-expression. Using material from nature or pen and paper, invest a moment to allow your mind the freedom to express, without judgment, in a safe and supportive environment.

12:00 - Intro to Ayurveda | Linn

Get a small insight into Ayurveda and learn simple tips that can really improve your daily life! Ayurveda is the most ancient but also the most practical health system of the world. Learn the first steps that can really improve your life:
- Improved digestion, less bloating & stomach pain
- Longer life in a good physical and psychical condition
- Stability in emotions, less stress and mental issues
- More joy!

13:00 - Japa Mala | Jelena

Make your own Japa Mala (beads) and practice with them - ‘Japa’ indicates a soft chanting of yoga sounds, or mantra. Repeating these mantra sounds have the effect of drawing the mind away from the material dimension with all its stress, worries, etc., an effect that cannot be achieved by any material thing. With this practice, gradually your mind and heart will develop toward more tolerance, care, and love for others.

14:00 - Drum Workshop | Andriy

Jam session and workshop of hand drumming - all kinds of hand drums, bring one if you have :) 
The program:
1) Whip technique in hand drumming (quick course to learn basic techniques of movement and hitting)
2) Developing your proper timing feeling (how to make GROOVE!)
3) Basic understanding how to create good drumming line (support and solo)
4) Jam session for all who have something to hit on
